Expert Network

We're building a network of people who know things.

Domain experts. PhD researchers. Industry veterans. If you've spent years earning deep expertise in a field, we want to work with you.

What the work looks like

Paid engagements.

Compensation for every validated interview or consultation. Rates reflect the depth of your expertise, not the shape of a generic survey. Your time has real value — we price it accordingly.

Flexible participation.

Async or live. Video, audio, or written. Fit it around your work, not the other way around. Review the brief, accept or decline — no obligation either way.

High-signal conversations.

You'll be matched to questions that belong in your domain. No generic surveys. No padding. The briefs are specific, the questions are real, and your answers shape how companies make decisions.

The process

Companies bring us questions they can't answer alone. Pricing thresholds they can't validate internally. Competitive dynamics they can't see from inside the org. Market signals they can collect but not interpret. These are the questions that reach our expert network.

We match those questions to qualified experts in our network. Not keyword matching — actual domain fit. If a company is trying to understand enterprise infrastructure buying behavior, that goes to people who have lived in that world. The match matters.

You review the brief and decide. Every engagement comes with a clear description of the question, the context, the format, and the compensation. You accept or decline. No obligation. No pressure. If the question isn't in your wheelhouse, it shouldn't reach you — and we work hard to make sure it doesn't.

If you engage, your insight shapes real decisions. Not academic research. Not a slide deck. The synthesis goes directly into pricing models, competitive strategy, and product roadmaps. What you know has commercial consequence — we take that seriously.
