The shift

AI adoption is no longer blocked by models. It’s blocked by implementation.

Most companies already have access to powerful models. What they lack is the infrastructure, expertise, workflows, and internal capacity to turn that capability into measurable operational improvement — and to keep it useful as the business changes. The hard part was never the model. It’s everything around it.

What we do

Pareto turns business operations into continuously improving AI systems.

We map how your company actually works — its knowledge, workflows, customers, and the judgment of the people who run it — bring in vetted domain experts, deploy practical AI into real workflows, and then keep improving those systems as your business changes. The result isn’t a pilot. It’s an operating system that gets better over time.
